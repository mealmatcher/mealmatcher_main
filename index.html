Elevator pitch:
Have you ever gone down to the dining hall but couldn't find any of your friends, so you ended up eating alone? MealMatcher provides the solution to that problem. Our service helps Princeton students find and share meals with new people. Just choose a day, a time, and a dining hall, and we'll anonymously and randomly match you with another student to eat with! We'll send you an email notification as soon as we find someone, along with a reminder an hour before. When it's time for your meal, show up at the dining hall card swipe and meet your meal buddy!

  • Locating the userid of the user you wish to disable under the auth_user table.
  • Locating the userprofile associated with that user under the mealmatcher_app_userprofile table. Then right click and select Edit Rows, and change disabled_status to True to disable the user. Then hit Save to lock-in the action. Please note that disabled_status: True disables the user, while disabled_status:False or NULL both allow the user to function normally.
